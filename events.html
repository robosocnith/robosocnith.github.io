DEUS EX MACHINA

Workshop on AI and IOT, organized by Robotics Society NITH on 28 & 29 September 2019 was successfully conducted, where in, approx 300 students participated. They learnt many new things and were able to build their AI bots.

RoboFiera

We successfully conducted an Online Workshop on Control of Mobile Robots on 26th Dec 2021. Approx 300 students participated and built their Castle Crusher on an online Simulator. They got to learn about basics of robotics too. Quizzeria 2.0, an online Quiz was conducted at the end.

Learn More

RoboScara

We successfully conducted an Online Workshop on Processing IDE on 9th Jan 2021. Approx 200 students participated and built their own robotic arm simulation. They got to learn about basics of robotics too. Quizzeria 1.0, an online Quiz was conducted at the end which was sponsored by LeafyPouch.

Robo Week

Roboweek is a week long event conducted by Robotics Society of NIT Hamirpur. The aim of ROBOWEEK is to spread awareness about robotics nation wide by providing a platform to Robotics Enthusiasts to learn and get their skills tested.
